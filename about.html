About Jazmin Collection

Home / About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2002, Jazmin Collection began as a small family business dedicated to preserving the traditional art of Jazmin weaving. What started as a passion project has grown into one of Pakistan's most respected textile houses, known for our authentic, high-quality Jazmin products.

Our Craftsmanship

Each piece in our collection is handwoven by master artisans who have inherited their skills through generations. We work directly with weavers in Kashmir and other traditional textile hubs to bring you the finest Jazmin shawls, fabrics, and accessories.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Preserving traditional weaving techniques
  • Supporting artisan communities
  • Providing customers with authentic, high-quality products
  • Promoting Pakistani craftsmanship worldwide
